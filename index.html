Our Story

Heritage of Taste

Spice Garden was born from a desire to preserve the rich culinary traditions of India while presenting them in a contemporary, elevated setting. Since 2010 we have been the cornerstone of fine dining in Bangalore.

Our chefs meticulously source every spice, grinding them in-house to ensure the profile of every dish is vibrant and authentic. We believe food is a celebration of life, family, and history.
